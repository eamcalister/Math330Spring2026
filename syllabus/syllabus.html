    Course Description.

    For Math 330:This course is an introduction to linear partial differential equations of order one and two. The primary applications are vibrations and heat flow in both bounded and unbounded domains. Integral transform and series methods are emphasized.
    ğŸ”—
    For Math 483: In addition to the topics of Math 330, students will prove rigorous analytic results on Fourier series/transforms to understand the subtle nature of solutions to PDE.
